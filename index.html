About US

Humidity & Temperature Sensor

To notify and raise awareness of plantino users about the humidity of their favourite plants & allowing the user to take action when the air humidity become extreme.

Soil Moisturity Sensor

Soil moisturity plays an essential role in plant growth. Thus, the device is present to regulate and maintain soil moisturity to achieve optimum growth.



Plantino

Light Intensity Sensor

The light intensity sensor captures the intensity of light projected onto the plant. Users are notified when extreme lighting or low lighting is detected.
